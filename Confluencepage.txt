Backend implementation for Perform Cascade functionality is largely completed, including:

- bulk cascade support
- immediate child cascade handling
- hierarchy validation
- cascaded from parent visibility
- cascade status handling
- actioned by/date updates
- duplicate prevention
- accept/reject review handling

“To Be Removed” downstream validation is partially completed and may require additional SIT/UAT validation.

Email notification functionality is currently out of scope and expected to be covered under separate email/reminder related stories.




Backend support for Metric Assignment screen is largely completed, including:

- Cascaded to Child retrieval
- status handling
- cascade date/actioned by
- metric applicability/name cascade/threshold cascade retrieval
- bulk and individual metric cascade support
- persistence of cascaded metrics after review actions
- review status tracking for greying out completed entries

“To be Removed” downstream handling and Out-of-Cycle override/new-entry behavior may require additional SIT/UAT validation for edge cases.

Frontend-specific items such as tab rendering, checkbox UI, and grey-out styling are outside backend scope.
