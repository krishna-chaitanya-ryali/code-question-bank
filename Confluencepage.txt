Title:
Request for NAS Drive Setup for Metric Cascade Supporting Documents (UAT & Production)

---

Description:
As part of the Metric Auto Cascade enhancement in the RAP application, we require a centralized storage location (NAS drive) to store supporting documents uploaded by users.

These documents will be associated with cascade actions (e.g., acceptance, rejection, or exception justification) and may include files such as Excel sheets, emails, or other relevant attachments.

To support this functionality, we need a NAS drive to be provisioned and accessible from the application servers.

---

Requirement Details:

- Environment:
  
  - UAT
  - Production

- Purpose:
  Store user-uploaded supporting documents related to metric cascade actions.

- Access Requirement:
  
  - Read/Write access from application servers
  - Access for backend services (Python APIs)

- Suggested Directory Structure:
  
  /nas/rap/metric_cascade/
    ├── uat/
    └── prod/

- File Types (Expected):
  
  - .xlsx
  - .xls
  - .csv
  - .msg / .eml (if email uploads are enabled)
  - .pdf (optional)

- Estimated Usage:
  
  - Moderate volume (based on cascade activity)
  - Files will be retained for audit and tracking purposes

---

Additional Requirements:

- Ensure proper permissions (restricted access to application service accounts only)
- Provide mount details/path for integration
- Confirm any size limits or quotas
- Backup/retention policy (if applicable)

---

Dependencies:

- Required for upcoming implementation of cascade audit/logging and document attachment feature

---

Priority:
Medium (Required for upcoming sprint)
